Egg Toast

15 min

Start your day right with a simple yet delightful breakfast: Egg Toast. This dish combines the crispiness of golden toast with a perfectly runny egg on top, creating a satisfying meal that’s both nutritious and delicious. Let’s dive into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread (sourdough, whole grain, or white)
  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil or butter, for toasting
  • Fresh herbs (like chives or parsley) for garnish (optional)
  • Avocado or cheese (optional for topping)

Instructions

  1. Toast the Bread: Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add a little olive oil or butter and toast the bread slices until golden brown on both sides. Set aside.
  2. Cook the Eggs: In the same skillet, crack the eggs and cook them sunny-side up or to your desired doneness. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Assemble the Toast: Place the toasted bread on a plate and top each slice with a cooked egg.
  4. Garnish and Serve: If desired, add fresh herbs, avocado slices, or a sprinkle of cheese on top for extra flavor. Enjoy immediately!
